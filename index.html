Pair Hunt 3D

Game Overview

Pair Hunt 3D transforms simple object-matching into a full sensory delight. Your goal is simple — scan the scene, find identical items, and clear them away. But with hundreds of colorful, rotating 3D objects, it’s more satisfying than you think. Whether you’re playing for focus or to unwind, this game delivers the perfect balance of chill and challenge.

FAQ

  • Is Pair Hunt 3D kid-friendly?

    Yes! It's suitable for all ages — relaxing for adults, engaging for kids.

  • Does the game require an internet connection?

    Nope. You can play anytime, anywhere, offline.

  • Will new object sets be added?

    Yes! New themed packs are released regularly to keep the experience fresh.

  • Can I play in timed or untimed mode?

    Both are available — choose what suits your mood.

  • Is there a way to reset progress?

    Yes, from the settings you can start fresh whenever you want.
